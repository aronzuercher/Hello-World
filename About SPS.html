Swiss Post Solution

Swiss Post Solution (SPS) is a sub company of the Swiss Post AG. The main business of SPS is the document processing of other companies who want to outsource the process.

Fields of Business

  • Document Management Service
  • Business Process Services
  • Intelligent Automation

Document Management Services

The main Busines "Document Management Service" is structured in three subbusinesses.

  1. Mailroom Services
  2. Document Processing Services
  3. Output Management Services
